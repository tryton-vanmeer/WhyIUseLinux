# Why I Use Linux

I use Linux because it gives me freedom.

  • The freedom to own my hardware
  • The freedom to own my software

I use Linux because it gives me choice

  • The choice to choose from a variety of distros
  • The choice to choose what software I use (Terminal, File Manager, Browser, etc)

I use Linux because it's free

  • as in speech
  • and as in Beer

But what I love most about Linux is the amazing community!
